# Brain-inspired Officially Joined the Karmada Adopter Group

Karmada is thrilled to announce that [Brain-inspired](http://www.leinao.ai/) has officially joined the Karmada Adopter Group and becomes an important member of the open-source community. As an Incubating project under Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF), Karmada is committed to providing users with powerful multi-cluster management and scheduling capabilities to help enterprises achieve efficient application deployment and management in complex distributed environments. The joining of Brain-inspired will further enrich the ecology of the Karmada community and inject new impetus into the project's continued innovation.

## About Brain-inspired

<div align="center">
  <img src="img/brain-inspired.png" />
</div>

Brain-inspired (founded in 2017) is a National High-Tech Enterprise and a National-Level Enterprise specializing in brain-inspired intelligence technology R&D and applications. The company holds an industry-leading position in cutting-edge AI fields, including machine vision foundation models, few-shot learning, causal vision and reasoning, stable learning, and brain-inspired game-theoretic optimization and decision-making. These technologies are extensively applied across its three core business domains: Computing Infrastructure, Smart Energy, and Computing-Electricity-Carbon Coordination.

Guided by its mission to "Drive the implementation of advanced intelligent technologies and empower industrial digital and intelligent transformation," Brain-inspired continuously delivers innovative intelligent products and solutions, striving to build deep, end-to-end closed loops for AI applications in vertical industries. The company is committed to becoming both a globally leading intelligent energy services provider and a key contributor to the global AI ecosystem.

## About Karmada Adopter Group

As a vital link between Karmada and its adopters, Karmada Adopter Group is dedicated to creating a deeply integrated, open, and collaborative high-value platform that fosters efficient interaction and knowledge sharing among its members. Through technical support, co-created events, and the exchange of best practices, the Karmada User Group continuously empowers users to enhance their capabilities in multi-cloud management, driving the thriving development of the cloud-native, multi-cloud, and multi-cluster ecosystem. Its primary goals and functions include:

- **Knowledge Sharing**: Facilitating the exchange of experiences, challenges, and solutions among Karmada adopters.
- **Promoting Collaboration**: Providing a platform for adopters to work together, share ideas, and solve common problems.
- **User Support**: Offering resources, tutorials, and guidance to help adopters effectively utilize Karmada.
- **Feedback Collection**: Listening to user feedback to guide Karmada's future development.
- **Community Events**: Enhancing community engagement through regular meetups, webinars, and other activities.

To date, over 35 institutions and organizations globally have publicly used Karmada in production environments. For further use cases and detailed case studies, please visit: https://karmada.io/adopters

## Join Karmada Adopter Group

Any company using Karmada in production environments can apply to join the Group. Whether you are an end-user or a vendor, your participation is welcome.

**End-adopters**: Companies or organizations that use Karmada for multi-cloud or multi-cluster management within their internal IT infrastructure.
**Vendors**: Companies that integrate Karmada into their products or services to offer to other enterprises or organizations.

By joining the Karmada Adopter Group, you can connect with peers facing similar challenges, seeking solutions and development that are more suitable for you. Including:

- **Community Technical Support**: Including solution evaluation, daily operations, issue troubleshooting, version upgrades, and more.  
- **Increased Visibility**: More global exposure for your company and team.
- **Building Technical Influence**: Opportunities to co-create technical presentations at events like KubeCon and other online/offline conferences.
- **Staying Informed**: Timely updates on critical features, bug fixes, and security risks.
- **Top Talent Recruitment**: Leverage community channels for global talent recruitment.
- **Expanding Business Opportunities**: Establish potential business connections and collaborations with other members.

Currently, there are no strict requirements for community contributions to join the Group. However, we encourage members to actively participate in community activities and share experiences and insights. Please note that future requirements may include contributions such as code submissions, documentation, issue resolutions, or use case sharing to maintain membership.

Visit the Karmada Adopter Group application form and submit an issue to start your new cloud-native journey.
